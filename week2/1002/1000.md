## Description: ##
 Given two pair of integers (a1,b1) and (a2,b2),can you calculate the expanded form of (a1*x+b1)*(a2*x+b2)?

 (note that we use "x^2" to express x2 and omit "*")

 这次你需要考虑系数为0的情况，即： 2x^2+0x+1, 这样应该写成: 2x^2+1


### input ###
1 0
1 0

### output ###
x^2
